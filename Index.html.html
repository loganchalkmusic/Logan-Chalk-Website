About Logan Chalk

BIO

Logan Chalk is a rising country artist from Parkton, Maryland, and is quickly making a name for himself in the music scene. A 2023 graduate from Hereford High School and current Towson University student, Logan balances his studies with a fast-growing music career.

He's shared the stage with some of Nashville's best, having performed alongside Riley Green and opened for acclaimed acts including Avery Anna, Jimmie Allen, Wade Hayes, and Alexandria Kay.

Influenced by artists like Morgan Wallen, Florida Georgia Line, and Riley Green, Logan continues to push the genre of Bro Country to new heights.

Catch Logan at upcoming shows and be sure to give him a follow.
